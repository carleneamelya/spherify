What's Carbon Footprint?

A carbon footprint is the total amount of carbon dioxide (CO₂) and other greenhouse gases that are released into the atmosphere because of our activities. These activities could be things like driving a car, using electricity, or making and using products.
It’s like measuring the total amount of "pollution" we generate through our daily actions, such as driving cars, using electricity, or producing waste. The larger our carbon footprint, the more we contribute to the accumulation of greenhouse gases, which accelerates climate change and its harmful impacts on the planet.
